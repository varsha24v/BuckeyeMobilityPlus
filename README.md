# CampusMobility+

## Overview
CampusMobility+ is a multi-functional platform tailored for college students, integrating ride-sharing, routing with AI-driven optimization, and safety heating maps that show areas of crime and reported suspicious behaviors.

### Features
- **Ride-sharing:** Find students heading in the same direction and pick them up. Carpooling is an eco-friendly and sustainable approach to improving the mobility of college students.
- **Efficient Routing:** Get recommendations for biking and walking on the fastest routes through APIs from Google Maps .
- **Safety Heat Maps:** Know the safest routes on campus with real-time updates.
